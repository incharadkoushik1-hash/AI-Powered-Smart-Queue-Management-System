## Notes

1. **Camera Resolution**: Minimum 720p recommended; 1080p preferred for better detection accuracy
2. **Raspberry Pi Alternatives**: Jetson Nano, Google Coral can be used for edge AI but increase cost significantly
3. **Webcam Selection**: Choose cameras with auto-focus and wide-angle lens for better coverage
